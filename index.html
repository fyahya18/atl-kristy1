About Kristy!

Kristy is 16 years old and lives in Columbus, Georgia. She's always been interested in dance and woahs everyday as a wake-up ritual. She has just now decided to pursue her dreams of becoming a hip hop dancer. Also, she has a puppy named Nala who is 4 months old!

Hobbies and Skills:

Kristy is well-versed in hitting the woah, but in her free time she loves to record videos for TikTok! She has been TikTok famous multiple times. She's also classically trained in other dance styles such as interpretive and tap dancing! She's also been known to sing a few tunes!
